About

A little bit about myslef.

I am a UX/UI Designer based in Sydney Australia. I thrive creating experiences for everyone to enjoy everyday.


I studied Nursing which led me to work for a medical device start-up as a global product consultant. During this period, I worked with customers on a daily basis and learnt from their interactions with our product. This helped me to gain valuable lessons about customer's interactions with graphic user interfaces. Then I moved into the educational field where I created online learning modules for medical professionals and maintained a learning management system, which led me to love UX/UI design even more.


Currently, I am testing and supporting enhancement of a medical product UI. This role has sharpened my skills in user-research and user-interface design even further.

Outside of design you can find me exploring nature, illustrating and teaching yoga.
